Privacy & Data Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Summary

We process your uploaded images to extract artist names. Images and results are stored temporarily. Artist names not found in our database are added as pending entries for review. We do not sell your data.

What Data We Collect

When you use the Festival Lineup Extractor, we collect and process:

  • Uploaded Images: Festival lineup poster images you submit for processing
  • Extracted Data: Artist names identified from your images
  • Generated Files: CSV files created from the extraction
  • Metadata: Festival name and year you provide

How We Use Your Data

  • Image Processing: Your images are sent to Anthropic's Claude AI for analysis to extract artist names
  • Database Matching: Extracted names are checked against our artist database
  • New Artist Discovery: Artists not found in our database are added to a pending review queue to help expand our artist catalog
  • File Storage: Uploaded images and generated CSVs are stored to provide download functionality

Data Storage & Retention

  • Uploaded images and CSV files are stored on our servers
  • Files may be retained for service improvement and debugging purposes
  • Pending artist entries are reviewed and may be added to our main database

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to provide this tool:

  • Anthropic (Claude AI): For image analysis and artist name extraction
  • Railway: For application hosting and database services
  • AWS CloudFront: For serving artist images

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request information about data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your uploaded images
  • Opt out of having extracted artists added to our pending queue

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) and secure database access controls.

Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or data requests, contact us at privacy@knokr.com.
